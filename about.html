Who am I?

I am a fourth-year Creative Media and Game Technologies student at Saxion University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands, with international experience from an Erasmus exchange at Pompeu Fabra University in Barcelona. My fascination with philosophy, audio, and technology has shaped both my studies and creative work. I see every creative product as a vision meant to be experienced through the senses. In audio, I find a powerful outlet to evoke designed emotions, whether through linear or interactive sound. I approach challenges as opportunities to grow, refine my craft, and push the boundaries of creative design and development.
